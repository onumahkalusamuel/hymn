I WON'T HAVE TO CROSS JORDAN ALONE

1. When I come to the river at the ending of day, When the last winds of sorrow have blown; There'll be somebody waiting to show me the way, I won't have to cross Jordan alone.

I won't have to cross Jordan alone Jesus died all my sins to atone In the darkness I see He'll be waiting for me I won't have to cross Jordan alone

2. Often times I'm weary and weary and sad, When it seems that my friends have all gone; There is one thought that cheers me and makes my heart glad, I won't have to cross Jordan alone.

I won't have to cross Jordan alone Jesus died all my sins to atone In the darkness I see He'll be waiting for me I won't have to cross Jordan alone

3. Tho' the billows of trouble and sorrow may sweep, Christ the Saviour will care for His own; Till the end of my journey my soul He will keep, And I won't have to cross Jordan alone.
