74

THE HEART THAT WAS BROKEN FOR ME

1. There came from the skies In the days long ago The Lord with a message of love; The world knew Him not; He was treated with scorn This wonderful gift from above

They crowned Him with thorns He was beaten with stripes He was smitten and nailed to the tree But the pain in His heart Was the hardest to bear The heart that was broken for me...

2. He came to His own - To the ones that He loved; The sheep that had wandered astray; They heard not His voice, But the Friend of mankind Was hated and driven away

They crowned Him with thorns He was beaten with stripes He was smitten and nailed to the tree But the pain in His heart Was the hardest to bear The heart that was broken for me...

3. The birds have their nests, and the foxes have holes, But He had no place for His head; A pallet of stone on the cold mountain side Was all that He had for His bed.

They crowned Him with thorns He was beaten with stripes He was smitten and nailed to the tree But the pain in His heart Was the hardest to bear The heart that was broken for me...

4. I cannot reject such a Saviour as he; Dishonour and wound Him again; I'll go to His feet and repent of my sin, Be willing to suffer the pain.

They crowned Him with thorns He was beaten with stripes He was smitten and nailed to the tree But the pain in His heart Was the hardest to bear The heart that was broken for me...
