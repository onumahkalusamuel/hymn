I WANT TO SEE JESUS, DON'T YOU?

1. There is One loved me so that for me he died, He's my dear, precious Saviour so true; On the cross for my sins He was crucified; I want to see Jesus, don't you?

I want to see Jesus, don't you? My Saviour so faithful and true;... When I reach the strand of that love-bright land, O I want to see Jesus, don't you?...

2. When I'm weary and faint He is always near, With his joy He my strength doth renew; And He comforts my heart, speaking words of cheer: I want to see Jesus, don't you?

I want to see Jesus, don't you? My Saviour so faithful and true;... When I reach the strand of that love-bright land, O I want to see Jesus, don't you?...

3. Holy angels keep watch o'er me thro' the night, And each morning He guards me anew; In the smile of His love doth my soul delight; I want to see Jesus, don't you?

I want to see Jesus, don't you? My Saviour so faithful and true;... When I reach the strand of that love-bright land, O I want to see Jesus, don't you?...

4. He is fairer than lily or rose to me, And His blessings fall soft as the dew; O my heart, how it longs His dear face to see: I want to see Jesus, don't you?

I want to see Jesus, don't you? My Saviour so faithful and true;... When I reach the strand of that love-bright land, O I want to see Jesus, don't you?...

5. There's a place for my soul that He doth prepare, And its beauty by faith I can view; First of all, when I enter that mansion fair, I want to see Jesus, don't you?

I want to see Jesus, don't you? My Saviour so faithful and true;... When I reach the strand of that love-bright land, O I want to see Jesus, don't you?...
