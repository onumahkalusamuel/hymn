219

MORE HOLINESS GIVE ME, MORE STRIVING WITHIN

1. More holiness give me, more striving within; More patience in suffering, more sorrow for sin; More faith in my Saviour, more sense of His care; More joy in His service, more purpose in prayer.

2. More gratitude give me, more trust in the Lord; More zeal for His glory, more hope in His Word; More tears for His sorrows, more pain at His grief; More meekness in trial, more praise for relief.

3. More purity give me, more strength to o'ercome; More freedom from earth-stains, more longings for home; More fit for the kingdom, more used would I be; More blessed and holy, more, Saviour, like Thee.
