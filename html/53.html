53

SO SEND I YOU

1. So send I you - to labour unrewarded To serve unpaid, unloved, unsought, unknown To bear rebuke, to suffer scorn and scoffing So send I you - to toil for Me alone

"As the Father hath sent Me, so send I you."

2. So send I you - to bind the bruised and broken O'er wandering souls to work, to weep, to wake To bear the burdens of a world a-weary So send I you - to suffer for My sake

"As the Father hath sent Me, so send I you."

3. So send I you - to loneliness and longing With heart a-hungering for the loved and known Forsaking home and kindred, friend and dear one So send I you - to know My love alone

"As the Father hath sent Me, so send I you."

4. So send I you - to leave your life's ambition To die to dear desire, self-will resign To labour long and love where men revile you So send I you - to lose your life in Mine

"As the Father hath sent Me, so send I you."

5. So send I you - to hearts made hard by hatred To eyes made blind because they will not see To spend, tho' it be blood to spend and spare not So send I you - to taste of Calvary

"As the Father hath sent Me, so send I you."
