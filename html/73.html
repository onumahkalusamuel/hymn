JESUS ONLY IS OUR MESSAGE

1. Jesus only is our message, Jesus all our theme shall be; We will lift up Jesus ever, Jesus only will we see.

Jesus only, Jesus ever, Jesus all in all we sing; Saviour, Sanctifier, and Healer, Baptizer and coming King.

2. Jesus only is our Saviour, All our guilt He bore away, All our righteousness He gives us, All our strength from day to day.

Jesus only, Jesus ever, Jesus all in all we sing; Saviour, Sanctifier, and Healer, Baptizer and coming King.

3. Jesus is our Sanctifier, Cleansing us from self and sin, And with all His Spirit's fulness, Filling all our hearts within.

Jesus only, Jesus ever, Jesus all in all we sing; Saviour, Sanctifier, and Healer, Baptizer and coming King.

4. Jesus only is our Healer, All our sicknesses He bare, And His risen life and fullness, All His members still may share.

Jesus only, Jesus ever, Jesus all in all we sing; Saviour, Sanctifier, and Healer, Baptizer and coming King.

5. Jesus only is our Power, He the Gift of Pentecost, Jesus, breathe Thy power upon us, Fill us with the Holy Ghost.

Jesus only, Jesus ever, Jesus all in all we sing; Saviour, Sanctifier, and Healer, Baptizer and coming King.

6. And for Jesus we are waiting, Listening for the trumpet's sound; Then it will be us and Jesus, Living ever with our God.

Jesus only, Jesus ever, Jesus all in all we sing; Saviour, Sanctifier, and Healer, Baptizer and coming King.
