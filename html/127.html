ROCK OF AGES CLEFT FOR ME

1. Rock of Ages, cleft for me, Let me hide myself in thee; Let the water and the blood, From thy riven side which flowed, Be of sin the double cure; Cleanse from guiLt and power.

2. Not the labours of my hands Can fulfil thy law's commands; Could my zeal no respite know, Could my tears forever flow, All for sin could not atone; Thou must save, and Thou alone.

3. Nothing in my hand I bring, Simply to the cross I cling; Naked, come to thee for dress; Helpless, look to thee for grace; Foul, I to the fountain fly; Wash me, Saviour, or I die.

4. While I draw this fleeting breath, When my eyelids close in death, When I soar to worlds unknown, See thee on thy judgement throne, Rock of Ages, cleft for me, Let me hide myself in Thee.
