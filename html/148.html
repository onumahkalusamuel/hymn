148

NO TEARS IN HEAVEN

1. No tears in heaven, no sorrows given. All will be glory in that land; There'll be no sadness, all will be gladness, When we shall join that happy band.

No tears, no tears, no tears up there, Sorrow and pain will all have flown; No tears, no tears, no tears up there; No tears in heaven will be known.

2. Glory is waiting, waiting up yonder, Where we shall spend an endless day; There with our Saviour, we'll be forever, Where no more sorrow can dismay.

No tears, no tears, no tears up there, Sorrow and pain will all have flown; No tears, no tears, no tears up there; No tears in heaven will be known.

3. Some morning yonder, we'll cease to ponder O'er things this life has bro't to view; All will be clearer, loved ones be dearer In heav'n where all will be made new.

No tears, no tears, no tears up there, Sorrow and pain will all have flown; No tears, no tears, no tears up there; No tears in heaven will be known.
