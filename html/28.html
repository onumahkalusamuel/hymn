I'M LIVING IN CANANN

1. All that drew me I have left behind Here in Canaan better joys I find; Peace abiding, blessings unconfined, For I'm living in Canaan now.

I am living in Canaan now, Where the showers of blessing abound, Where the riches of grace in plenty are found, I am living in Canaan now

2.Safe abiding I will never fear, For my Saviour ever will be near; When I call Him He will always hear, For I'm living in Canaan now.

I am living in Canaan now, Where the showers of blessing abound, Where the riches of grace in plenty are found, I am living in Canaan now

3.I am drinking from a ceaseless well; Here in Canaan where I love to dwell, So to others I am glad to tell, That I'm living in Canaan now.

I am living in Canaan now, Where the showers of blessing abound, Where the riches of grace in plenty are found, I am living in Canaan now

4. Praises ever I am glad to bring Unto Jesus, my Redeemer King, For His mercies I will shout and sing, For I'm living in Canaan now.

I am living in Canaan now, Where the showers of blessing abound, Where the riches of grace in plenty are found, I am living in Canaan now
