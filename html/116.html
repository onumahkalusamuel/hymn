CARRY YOUR BIBLE

1. Carry your Bible with you, Let all its blessings outflow; It will supply you each moment Take it wherever you go!

Take it wherever you go, (you go,) Take it wherever you go, (you go;) God's message of love Sent down from above, Oh, take it wherever you go!

2. Carry the word of pardon, Sweeter each day it will grow; Somewhere some heart will be waiting Take it wherever you go!

Take it wherever you go, (you go,) Take it wherever you go, (you go;) God's message of love Sent down from above, Oh, take it wherever you go!

3. Carry the wondrous story, Tell it to hearts plung'd in woe; This word of gracious redemption Take it wherever you go!

Take it wherever you go, (you go,) Take it wherever you go, (you go;) God's message of love Sent down from above, Oh, take it wherever you go!

4. Carry the word of promise; Sinners unpardon'd may know God's path from sin unto safety Take it wherever you go!

Take it wherever you go, (you go,) Take it wherever you go, (you go;) God's message of love Sent down from above, Oh, take it wherever you go!
