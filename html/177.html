177

SEARCH ME, O GOD

1. Search me, O God, and know my heart today, Try me, O Lord, and know my thoughts, I pray; See if there be some wicked way in me; Cleanse me from ev'ry sin, and set me free.

2. I praise Thee, Lord, for cleansing me from sin; Fulfil Thy word and make me pure within; Fill me with fire, where once I burned with shame; Grant my desire to magnify Thy name.

3. Lord, take my life, and make it wholly Thine; Fill my poor heart with Thy great love divine; Take all my will, my passion, self and pride; I now surrender - Lord, in me abide.

4. O Holy Ghost, revival comes from Thee; Send a revival - start the work in me: Thy Word declares Thou wilt supply our need; For blessings now, O Lord, I humbly plead.
