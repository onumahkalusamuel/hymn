IS YOUR LIFE A CHANNEL OF BLESSING?

1. Is your life a channel of blessing? Is the love of God flowing thro' you? Are you telling the lost of the Saviour? Are you ready His service to do?

Make me a channel of blessing today, Make me a channel of blessing, I pray; My life possessing, my service blessing, Make me a channel of blessing today.

2. Is your life a channel of blessing? Are you burdened for those that are lost? Have you urged upon those who are straying, The Saviour who died on the cross?

Make me a channel of blessing today, Make me a channel of blessing, I pray; My life possessing, my service blessing, Make me a channel of blessing today.

3. Is your life a channel of blessing? Is it a daily telling for Him? Have you spoken the word of salvation To those who are dying in sin?

Make me a channel of blessing today, Make me a channel of blessing, I pray; My life possessing, my service blessing, Make me a channel of blessing today.

4. We cannot be channels of blessing If our lives are not free from all sin; We will barriers be and a hindrance To those we are trying to win.

Make me a channel of blessing today, Make me a channel of blessing, I pray; My life possessing, my service blessing, Make me a channel of blessing today.
