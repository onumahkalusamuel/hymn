GO AND TELL THEM

1. Send the gospel of salvation, To a world of dying men; Tell it out to every nation, 'Till the Lord shall come again.

Go and tell them, go and tell them, Jesus died for sinful men. Go and tell them, go and tell them, He is coming, He is coming, He is coming back again.

2. 'Tis the church's great commission, 'Tis the Master's last command; Christ has died for every creature, Tell it out in every land.

Go and tell them, go and tell them, Jesus died for sinful men. Go and tell them, go and tell them, He is coming, He is coming, He is coming back again.

3. Christ is gathering out a people To His name from every race; Haste to give the invitation Ere shall end the day of grace.

Go and tell them, go and tell them, Jesus died for sinful men. Go and tell them, go and tell them, He is coming, He is coming, He is coming back again.

4. Give the gospel as a witness, To a world of sinful men; Till the Bride shall be completed, And the Lord shall come again

Go and tell them, go and tell them, Jesus died for sinful men. Go and tell them, go and tell them, He is coming, He is coming, He is coming back again.
