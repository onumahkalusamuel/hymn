GUIDE ME, O THOU GREAT JEHOVAH

1. Guide me, O Thou great Jehovah, Pilgrim through this barren land. I am weak, but Thou art mighty; Hold me with Thy powerful hand. Bread of Heaven!(2ce) Feed me now and ever more(2ce)

2. Open now the crystal fountain, Whence the healing stream doth flow; Let the fire and cloudy pillar Lead me all my journey through. Strong Deliverer!(2ce) Be Thou still my Strength and Shield.(2ce)

3. If I tread the verge of Jordan, Bid my anxious fears subside: Bear me through the swelling torrent, Land me safe on Canaan's side: Songs of praises!(2ce) I will ever give to Thee.(2ce)

4. Saviour, come! we long to see Thee, Long to dwell with Thee above; And to know in full communion, All the sweetness of Thy love. Come, Lord Jesus! (2ce) Take Thy waiting people home. (2ce)
