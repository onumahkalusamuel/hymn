18

BLESSED ASSURANCE

1. Blessed assurance, Jesus is mine! Oh, what a fore-taste of glory divine! Heir of salvation, purchase of God, Born of His Spirit, wash'd in His blood.

This is my story, this is my song, Praising my Saviour all the day long; This is my story, this is my song, Praising my Saviour all the day long.

2. Perfect submission, perfect delight, Visions of rapture now burst on my sight, Angels descending, bring from above Echoes of mercy, whispers of love.

This is my story, this is my song, Praising my Saviour all the day long; This is my story, this is my song, Praising my Saviour all the day long.

3. Perfect submission, all is at rest, I in my Saviour am happy and blest; Watching and waiting, looking above, Fill'd with His goodness, lost in His love.

This is my story, this is my song, Praising my Saviour all the day long; This is my story, this is my song, Praising my Saviour all the day long.
