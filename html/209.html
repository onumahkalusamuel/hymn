I WILL NOT FORGET THEE

1. Sweet is the promise "I will not forget thee," Nothing can molest or turn my soul away; E'en though the night be dark within the valley, Just beyond is shining an eternal day.

I. . . will not forget thee or leave thee; In My hands I'll hold thee, In My arms I'll fold thee, I. . . will not forget thee or leave thee; I am thy Redeemer, I will care for thee.

2. How can I show my gratitude to Jesus, For His love unfailing and His tender care? I will proclaim to others His salvation, That they may accept Him and His promise share.

I. . . will not forget thee or leave thee; In My hands I'll hold thee, In My arms I'll fold thee, I. . . will not forget thee or leave thee; I am thy Redeemer, I will care for thee.

3. Trusting the promise "I will not forget thee," Onward I will go with songs of joy and praise; Tho' earth despise me, tho' my friends forsake me, Jesus will be near me gladdening my days.

I. . . will not forget thee or leave thee; In My hands I'll hold thee, In My arms I'll fold thee, I. . . will not forget thee or leave thee; I am thy Redeemer, I will care for thee.

4. When at the golden portals I am standing, All my tribulations, all my sorrows past; How sweet to hear the blessed proclamation, "Enter, faithful servant, welcome home at last!"

I. . . will not forget thee or leave thee; In My hands I'll hold thee, In My arms I'll fold thee, I. . . will not forget thee or leave thee; I am thy Redeemer, I will care for thee.
